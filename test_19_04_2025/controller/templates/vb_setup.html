VB-Audio Cable Setup

1. Click the button to open your Windows sound settings.

2. In the Playback tab, select CABLE Input and click Set Default.

3. In the Recording tab, select CABLE Output and click Set Default.

4. Double-click on CABLE Output to open its properties.

5. In the Properties window, go to the Listen tab.
Check the box "Listen to this device".
In Playback through this device, select your headphones or speakers.

6. Click Apply and then OK.
Back in the Sound window, click Apply and OK again.

7. Once you're done, click the button below to complete the setup.
